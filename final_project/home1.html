Our Ingredients:

From handpicked apples to sun-ripened tomatoes and golden pumpkins, each ingredient is thoughtfully selected to ensure peak quality and taste. Our commitment to freshness not only enhances the natural flavors of our creations but also brings a wholesome, seasonal touch to every dish and beverage we serve. With every bite, you'll experience the vibrant essence of nature's finest, transformed into culinary delights crafted with care and creativity.

Rich Pumpkins:

With a smooth, tender texture and a subtle nutty finish, green pumpkins bring a unique and delightful richness to pastries and drinks, elevating them with a natural sweetness and earthy warmth. In our cafe, this versatile ingredient takes center stage in some of our most beloved treats. Imagine biting into a flaky pumpkin croissant, its golden layers filled with a spiced pumpkin cream that melts in your mouth, or savoring a slice of our pumpkin chai loaf cake, infused with aromatic spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and nutmeg.

Sun-Kissed Tomatoes:

Savor the taste of the garden with every bite of our perfectly ripe tomatoes, where their delicate balance of sweet and tangy notes adds a vibrant burst of flavor to our pastries and drinks. Enjoy them in flaky tomato tarts, filled with roasted heirloom tomatoes and fresh herbs, or in savory scones where their juiciness pairs beautifully with sharp cheese. For a refreshing twist, try our tomato basil spritzer or the bold, creamy roasted tomato latte, blending unexpected flavors into every sip. Whether in a pastry or a drink, our tomatoes bring the sun-kissed essence of the garden to your table.
